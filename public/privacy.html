Privacy Policy — CyberSaathi

Effective date: October 28, 2025

CyberSaathi ("we", "our", "us") provides a chatbot and related services to help users with cyber safety and cybercrime guidance. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information we collect

  • User-provided data: messages you send to the chatbot (text only), optional contact details if you provide them for follow-up.
  • Device & usage data: basic device info, IP address, timestamps, and usage logs for service health and debugging.
  • No sensitive data collection: do not send passwords, credit card numbers, or highly sensitive personal health data. If you do, we may log it only to the extent necessary to investigate abuse but we do not intentionally store or process payment or health records.

2. How we use your information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the service (process chat messages, produce responses).
  • Improve and train our models (anonymized/aggregated logs may be used to improve service quality).
  • Detect and prevent abuse and fraud.

3. Data sharing & third parties

We may share data with:

  • Service providers (hosting on AWS, analytics, email delivery) who process data on our behalf.
  • Legal authorities when required by law, or to protect rights, property or safety.

4. Where data is stored & retention

Data is hosted on AWS (EC2) in the region specified in our infrastructure. We retain logs and chatbot data for troubleshooting and product improvement; retention periods vary but we aim to minimize storage time. Contact us to request deletion or export.

5. Security

We use standard security practices (HTTPS/TLS, server-side updates, restricted access) to protect data. However no system is 100% secure; in case of a breach we will notify affected users and authorities as required by law.

6. Children

The service is not intended for children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected personal information of a child, contact us to request deletion.

7. Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or export of your data. To exercise rights, contact: shocencops@gmail.com.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or requests: shocencops@gmail.com

By using CyberSaathi, you consent to this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy; we will post the date of last revision here.
