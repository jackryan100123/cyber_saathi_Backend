Police & Helplines — CyberSaathi

Use these resources for immediate cybercrime assistance and reporting.

Chandigarh Cyber Helpline

Phone: 1930 or 0172-2749900

Online: cybercrime.gov.in

What to do if you're a victim

  1. Immediately call 1930 or local police if there's active harm.
  2. Preserve evidence: screenshots, message logs, transaction IDs.
  3. Do not delete messages or change device settings before reporting.
  4. Contact your bank if financial loss occurred.

Non-emergency reporting

Report scams, fraud, or phishing at: cybercrime.gov.in
